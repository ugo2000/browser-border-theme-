Make your browser come alive with dynamic colored borders that pulse and react to the music you're playing.

🎵 Page Audio Reactive — Borders respond to any audio or video playing in your browser. No microphone permission needed.

🎨 8 Beautiful Themes — Neon, Ocean, Fire, Forest, Sunset, Aurora, Candy, Gold

✨ 5 Animation Effects — Breathe, Rainbow, Pulse, Wave, Aurora

🔒 Privacy First — No data collection, no tracking, works entirely locally, no server communication.

How to use:

Pin the extension to your toolbar
Click ON to activate borders
Choose a theme and effect
Enable Page Audio and play music — watch your browser edges dance!
